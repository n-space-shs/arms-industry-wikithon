arms industry wikithon

The arms industry wikithon is a monthly meeting dedicated to expanding and updating public-domain information about companies, entities and individuals involved in the global arms industry. We meet online and in person to share resources, knowledge and editing guidance.

No prior knowledge of either the arms industry or Wikipedia editing is required to attend, though both are also welcomed. Full support and an introduction to Wikipedia editing will be provided. Participants should bring a laptop that can connect to the internet.

Our first meeting will be at Newspeak House on Thursday February 12th, from 6:30-9:30pm London time. You can sign up to attend in person here, or join remotely using the link here.
